Our Mission

Stay informed.
Our team hopes to provide a simple and user-friendly web application to help Prince George’s County residents find their local polling centers. We encourage residents to use this tool to locate their closest polling center and participate in upcoming elections.

About Us

Our small team of developers from the University of Maryland is dedicated to helping Prince George’s County residents exercise their voting rights. Our project utilizes our programming and design skills gained from the university’s College of Information Studies to provide a working product for public use. For more information, please visit the program website.

Why?

Stay involved.
The 2016 presidential election had one of the lowest voter turnout rates in the past two decades – and we’re quickly approaching another crucial election year. While Maryland has seen a slight increase in voter turnout for the 2018 midterm election, it is important to ensure that voters take the first step in by locating their polling centers. Our application improves upon existing poll center finders and provides an easy means to getting informed.
